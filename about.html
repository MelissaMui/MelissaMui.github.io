Hi! Nice to meet you, I'm Melissa

Born Nga Hei Mui, Melissa is from Hong Kong but was raised in Beijing, China, where she gained her Third Culture Kid (TCK) identity. This unique life experience is a major part of her image-making process. Through the use of photography, video and archival materials, Melissa often discusses the complexities of her identity by drawing from personal experiences. Themes of self-discovery, family dynamics and artificial intelligence are explored within her work.

She is currently based in New York City.
